About

KATALYST-DIGITAL

KATALYST-DIGITAL helps teams move away from scattered spreadsheets and manual processes into simple, reliable digital tools. Most of the work sits at the intersection of:

  • • Digital insurance & claims workflows
  • • Retail analytics & inventory monitoring
  • • Automotive / fleet sales and operations
  • • Legal and professional services websites

The goal is always the same: build something your team is actually happy to use, not just something that looks good in a slide deck.

How projects usually run

We start with a short call and a few concrete examples of the problems you’re facing. From there we agree on a small first milestone (often 2–3 weeks) to build something real you can interact with.

You get frequent check-ins, Loom/Zoom walkthroughs, and clear summaries so that non-technical stakeholders stay in the loop.
