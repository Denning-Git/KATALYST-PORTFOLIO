About KATALYST-DIGITAL

KATALYST-DIGITAL helps teams move away from scattered spreadsheets and manual follow-ups into simple, reliable digital tools. The work sits at the intersection of product thinking, clean UX, and understanding how African businesses actually operate on the ground.

What I focus on

  • • Digital insurance & claims workflows
  • • Retail analytics & inventory monitoring
  • • Automotive / fleet sales and operations tools
  • • Legal and professional services websites

The goal is always to ship something your team is comfortable using, that reduces stress instead of becoming “one more system” people avoid.

How we usually work together

Projects typically start with a short call and a few real examples from your current workflow. From there we pick a small but valuable first milestone: often a working prototype or a focused feature we can deploy and test quickly.

You get regular updates, short written summaries, and room for feedback as we build.
